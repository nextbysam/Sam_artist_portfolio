Stateless Obsession

I call this "stateless." It's just being human. Ironically, A lot of people suck at being human.

I believe in being fully human. Let everything flow through you. Desire, suffering, beauty, rejection - without letting it control you. I personally don't like to split myself into a spiritual self that transcends the messy stuff and an ego self that experiences it. I'm one person(and hopefully you too).

But there's one exception. One thing you obsess about mercilessly. Something your gut tells you matters. Something that only your bone can feel when you think about it. You can devote your entire life to it. You can be obsessed and fully human at the same time.

I like to use a framework: if everyone started doing this thing, would it push humanity forward or hold us back? I want to be a catalyst, not an inhibitor.

I believe in creating, not competing.

Competition is for those who accept the status quo.

I aim for the revolutionary:

solving humanity's deepest problems, not playing the game better.

Like Steve Jobs, Tesla, Da Vinci.

The ones who see what doesn't exist yet.

Be human and Be Obsessed.

Here's to the crazy ones.

The misfits.

The rebels.

The troublemakers.

The round pegs in the square holes.

The ones who see things differently.

They're not fond of rules.

And they have no respect for the status quo.

You can praise them, disagree with them, quote them, disbelieve them, glorify or vilify them.

About the only thing you can't do is ignore them.

Because they change things.

They invent. They imagine. They heal.

They explore. They create. They inspire.

They push the human race forward.

Maybe they have to be crazy.

How else can you stare at an empty canvas and see a work of art?

Or sit in silence and hear a song that's never been written?

Or gaze at a red planet and see a laboratory on wheels?

We make tools for these kinds of people.

While some see them as the crazy ones, we see genius.

Because the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world, are the ones who do.
